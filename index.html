Drone

The DJI Mavic Air features a fully stabilized 3-axis gimbal camera capable of shooting 4K video and 12 megapixel photos. The maximum flight time of the drone is 21 minutes.

Technical Parameters

AIRCRAFT

Dimensions: 168×184×64 mm (L×W×H), Weight: 430 g, Internal Storage: 8 GB


CAMERA

Sensor: 1/2.3” CMOS 12 MP, Lens: 24 mm, f/2.8, Image Size: 4056×3040 px, Video Resolution: 4K Ultra HD: 3840×2160, Photo Format: JPEG/DNG (RAW)


BATERRY

Capacity: 2375 mAh, Max Charging Power: 60 W, Battery Type: LiPo 3S
